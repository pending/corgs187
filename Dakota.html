Adopt / Corgi Profiles / Dakota

Dakota

Age: 2 Years

Gender: Female

Breed: Corgi Mix & Shetland Sheepdog Sheltie

About: Meet Dakota! Dakota is an extremely affectionate corgi mix. Favorite pass times -- being hugged and sitting in your lap! She is a happy dog, playful, and energetic. But she does know her manners. She loves other dogs, even cats. She would be a wonderful companion for children as she is quite gentle. She is up to date on shots, neutered, and house broken. She was picked up as a stray, so we don't know about her background. But she is a sweetie that can move right into a new loving home.

Fee: $105

Location: SD Humane Society Oceanside Campus
