Support Us / Volunteer

changing lives one corgi at a time

we need you

The work we've been able to do because of donations and the help of volunteers has transformed the lives of not only the corgis, but also the families who decided to adopt them.

Here's how to start volunteering:

1.

Be at least 16 years of age, and be able to commit at least one year of volunteering.

2.

Explore our volunteer opportunities. Come into one our shelters and take a gander!

3.

Complete an application, participate in an online information session, and interview with a member of our team.

4.

Get started changing lives!
