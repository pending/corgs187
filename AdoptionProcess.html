Adopt / Adoption Process

Here is how the process works:

Step 1 : Explore the corgis currently available for adoption

You can look at the corgis up for adoption here. We keep this updated as corgis are adopted or become available. Check back often!

Step 2 : Visit one of our adoption centers to meet potential pets

Once you have found an animal that you are interested in adopting, contact a volunteer or staff member about visiting with the pet. To ensure the pet is compatible with everyone, all household/family members should interact with the animal before the adoption is finalized. If you are interested in adopting one of our Hidden Gems, call to schedule a visit.

Step 3 : Complete an adoption application and interview

To begin the adoption process, you must agree to our adoption policies and complete an adoption application form. We will also review with you any medical or behavioral information relevant to that animal, explain policies and services available to adopters, and collect adoption fees.

Step 4 : Welcome your new family member home

We want to provide services for all stages of a corgi's life. Our website also has many resources to help pet owners including microchipping, spay/neuter, pet friendly housing and a lost pet bulletin board.
