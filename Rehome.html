Rehome

Do you really need to rehome your corgi? Maybe some of our resources can help you decide.

Behavior Problems

Behavior problems in Corgis is something that can be managed or fixed with some basic obedience training and increasing its daily exercise.

Housing Problems

Can't find an apartment or house that allows pets? We can point you to some affordable pet-friendly housing!

Military Problems

Are you about to go out for deployment? Is your military life making it hard for you to keep a pet? We may have some solutions for you.

Financial Problems

Is your corgi getting too expensive for you to keep? There are a list of pet organizations that can help you afford dog food and other dog miscellanious things!

Rehome Process

If your corgi doesn't have any of the other problems listed, or you are unable to resolve the issues you have with your corgi, then we can direct you to shelters in the San Diego area for rehoming.
